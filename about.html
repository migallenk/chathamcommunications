Who We Are


Launched in 2016, Chatham Communications is an award-winning communications and consulting company boldly transforming the way the world thinks about dessert. We harness communications, art and design to establish unique selling propositions and storytelling. Clients come to Chatham when they want to create a better story that makes a difference, and tell it in a way that achieves their objectives through measurable results. Chatham Communications brings together the right mix of talented, seasoned and motivated professionals — from world renowned chefs, digital strategists and food photographers to food writers, critics and connoisseurs — to shape a new food frontier with our clients.
